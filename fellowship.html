FELLOWSHIP PROGRAMME

Honouring the life and legacy of Dr Krishnaswamy Kasturirangan (1940-2025), a visionary scientist, space pioneer, and key architect of India's National Education Policy 2020. This fellowship supports emerging scholars who reflect his spirit of innovation, interdisciplinary thinking, and commitment to national development through knowledge and education.

Apply now
Applications open : October 25, 2026
ABOUT THE FELLOWSHIP

Objective

Supports outstanding candidates with leadership potential driven by the need to improve, further, and advance existing methods and systems.
Specifically targets individuals working on cross-disciplinary, interdisciplinary, or multidisciplinary topics that connect science, engineering, humanities, and social sciences to address major educational and societal challenges.
